About Us

Karachi, the Crown of Pakistan, thrives on growth and opportunity — and construction lies at the heart of its progress. Since 1962, M/S Lateef Builders, established by Mr. Muhammad Abdul Lateef (Late), has shaped that journey through vision, craftsmanship, and integrity.

From high-rise landmarks to modern homes, every project reflects our dedication to quality, innovation, and timely delivery. With every brick, we strengthen our promise of building a better city — one project, one family, and one legacy at a time.

At Lateef Builders, we don’t just build structures — we build trust, reputation, and a lasting legacy of quality.
