About Lateef Builders & Properties

Lateef Properties is the real estate arm of M/S Lateef Builders, a trusted name in Karachi’s construction and development industry since 1962. Built on a foundation of integrity, excellence, and customer trust, Lateef Properties extends this legacy into the world of property sales, investments, and management.

Specializing in residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments, we offer clients a curated selection of high-quality properties. Backed by decades of industry experience, Lateef Properties is committed to transparency, value, and lasting relationships — helping people and businesses find spaces where they can truly thrive.
